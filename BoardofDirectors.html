Ethics of AI Institute Board of Directors

The Ethics of AI Institute is governed by its Board of Directors according to our organization's charter.

Matthew S.W. Silk

Executive Director

Matthew Silk is the Executive Director of the Ethics of AI Institute. He is the co-author of An Inquirer's Guide to Ethics in AI. He also founded the original Ethics of AI Society of Waterloo and is the host of the Ethics of AI Podcast.

Samantha St. Amand

Secretary/Treasurer

Samantha St. Amand serves as Secretary/Treasurer on the Board of Governors of the Ethics of AI Institute. She is a Data Scientist at the University of Waterloo, with over a decade of experience in data analytics and statistical modelling. Her background in economics, global governance research, and institutional data integration informs her commitment to multi-disciplinary, evidence-based evaluation of the societal implications of emerging technologies. Samantha holds an MA in Economics from the University of Waterloo, a BA in Economics from Wilfrid Laurier University, a BA in French Studies from the University of Waterloo, and is currently pursuing an MMSc in Management of Technology at the University of Waterloo, deepening her focus on the intersection of technology, governance, and responsible innovation.

Kem-Laurin Lubin

Director-at-large

Kem completed her PhD in English at the University of Waterloo. She focuses on AI biases and their ongoing effect on the lived experience of people, particularly the marginalized.
