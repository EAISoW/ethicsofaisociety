Panel Discussion

Panelists
Rob Cerjanec, MPP for Ajax
Rob Cerjanec is the Member of Provincial Parliament for Ajax and Ontario Liberal Critic for Economic Development & Innovation, and Tourism, Sport & Culture. A leading voice on AI policy, he has introduced legislation to strengthen Ontario’s approach to artificial intelligence and champions the commercialization of homegrown innovation to drive growth, create jobs, and expand exports.
Dr. Patricia Marino, Professor of Philosophy, University of Waterloo
Patricia Marino is a Professor of Philosophy at the University of Waterloo.
Dr. Ian J. MacDonald, Philosopher, University of Waterloo
Ian MacDonald is an educator and writer at the University of Waterloo who investigates the interrelations between inquiry, meaning, and method, with a focus on AI ethics, the ethics of belief, and American pragmatism. He is co-author, with Matt Silk, of An Inquirer’s Guide to Ethics in AI.
